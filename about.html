About Mariana: A Journey of Passion and Pursuit

Mariana, a spirited 27-year-old originally from Ukraine, embarked on a life-altering journey two years ago when she ventured to the USA. This pivotal move marked the beginning of a remarkable odyssey that has seen her traverse the realms of finance, entrepreneurship, and creative expression.

With a background in finance and banking, Mariana found her professional niche as a product supply specialist at Frito-Lay, where she's immersed in the intricacies of logistics and innovation. Fluent in three languages, she brings a multicultural perspective to her work, enriching her interactions and broadening her horizons.

Yet, Mariana's story extends far beyond the boardroom. She's a fervent devotee of photography, aerial gymnastics, and yoga, each hobby offering a unique avenue for self-expression and fulfillment. Through the lens of her camera, she captures the beauty and essence of the world around her, while aerial gymnastics empowers her to defy gravity and express herself artistically. Yoga, meanwhile, serves as a sanctuary of peace and mindfulness amidst life's hustle and bustle.

In her pursuit of personal and professional growth, Mariana embraces a diverse range of learning approaches, from formal classes and workshops to self-study and experimentation. Her master's degree in finance has equipped her with the strategic acumen and financial prowess needed to navigate the complexities of entrepreneurship, laying the groundwork for her ultimate ambition—to establish her own real estate photography business.

Driven by an unwavering sense of purpose and a thirst for adventure, Mariana took the leap into entrepreneurship with gusto. Her decision to chart her own course and chase her dreams is a testament to her resilience and willingness to embrace change.

As she looks to the future, Mariana's spirit of exploration remains undimmed. With her sights set on elevating her real estate photography venture to new heights, she approaches each new chapter of her journey with optimism and excitement. For Mariana, the beauty lies not just in the destination, but in the adventure itself—the endless possibilities, the uncharted territories, and the joy of discovery along the way.
